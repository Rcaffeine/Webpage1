The family car can be a 21st century SUV, or a 1940s "tin can", or a Land Rover, or a 2 CV. Powerwise, we are talking F1, or GP car. Sportswise, Rallying or F1. I was talking to a neighbour who owns a 1940s Harley. We agreed that car design stopped being interesting in 2000. Let's not go into details. Power-wise, what is more exciting? A post-2000 WRC car, a pre-2007 F1, a pre-airfoil or post-airfoil GP car? WRC cars are interesting, but an 80s Rally has "de la gueule". A pre-airfoil GP or a shrill 2005 F1 V8?
